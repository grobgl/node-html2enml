Careers slots

 

This term, Richard Carruthers and Barnaby Mollett from the Careers Service will be running weekly 15 minute Careers Consultations and Internship Consultations for Computing students on Mondays, 13.00 – 14.00 in 219A Huxley (from Monday 11th January through to Monday 21st March 2016). These sessions provide you an opportunity to:

 

·         Ask any career-related questions you have

·         Discuss applications and interviews

·         Get advice about planning your career, finding internships, or securing a graduate job

 

To attend, you can book an appointment slot via Jobslive – select either Careers or Internship Consultation (219A Huxley) – from 07.00 on the morning of the day of the appointment. The Careers Service team also run consultations open to all students daily, as well as hosting seminars, workshops and other events – visit imperial.ac.uk/careers for more details.
